Worldwide Population

This page is about the worldwide population in year 2020. It shows the total population of all countries in the world in 2020 as well as top religions, deaths and births, total CO2 emissions from countries.

The main purpose of preparing this visualisation is to illustrate the number of population in the world as well as other aspects like religions' popularity, number of deaths and carbon dioxide (CO2) emissions in which these things are not commonly considered by most people.

ℹ Comparison of Number of Disciples in Religions by Regions in Years 2010 to 2050

Click on the legend for highlight filter.
Interact with the slider to view year changes.

This chart shows the comparison and estimation of the number of disciples giving faith to different types of religions in the world.

From the chart, Christian is the most popular religion in the world in nowadays (year 2010). However, in the next 50 years (i.e. year 2050), Islam/Muslim would become one of the most popular religion.

ℹ Yearly Number of Deaths by Country in Years 2015 and 2022

Interact with the slider to change years.
Interact with the drop-down list to change countries.
If no data are shown, probably there's no data for the country chosen.

According to Karlinsky & Korbak from GitHub where the data is published on 2021, it is collected since the start of COVID-19 pandemic. This line chart is created to visualise the number of deaths in a comparison of years so that people can be aware with it.

In general, the number of deaths rises at the time when the pandemic starts and worsen, and it seems to become normal after that time.

Feel free to change the country and drag the year filter for comparison.
